What Are Quartz Countertops?

Quartz is one of the most reliable materials we use for kitchen and bathroom countertops. It is a permanent stone, which lasts a lifetime for customers.Additionally, quartz is also a flexible material that is easier to install. The strength, durability, and flexibility are due to a composition of 93% quartz and 7% resin. In fact, quartz is stronger than natural stones like granite and marble.Sophisticated yet practical, Quartz countertops are scratch, stain, and heat resistant. Eternally beautiful while impressively durable, Q Premium Natural Quartz is the latest in advanced quartz countertop surfaces from MSI.
